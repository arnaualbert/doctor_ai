Bioinformatics Tools

  1. Image Recognition

    This tool is able to detect both brain tumours and pneumonia. The main function is to give a quick response to the given image in order to be of help to the medical staff. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  2. Extract CDS

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing it a genbank from which it will extract the CDS inside. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  3. Genbank to Fasta

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing it a genbank from which it will extract the Fasta inside. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  4. DNA to Protein

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing a fasta file with DNA in it which will be converted to protein. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  5. DNA to RNA

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing a fasta file with DNA inside which will be converted to RNA. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  6. Complementary Sequence

    This tool is able to give a quick answer by providing a fasta file with DNA from which it will draw the respective complementary strand. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  7. Split Fasta

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing a fasta file with DNA and two numbers which will be the start of the cut and the end of the cut to be made in the chain. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  8. Global Alignment

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing two files in fasta format and is able to align them globally. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  9. Local Alignment

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing two files in fasta format and is able to align them locally. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  10. Reverse Complementary

    This tool is able to give a quick response by passing a fasta file with DNA from which it will draw the respective reverse complementary strand. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.

  11. Random Sequence

    This tool is able to give a quick answer by passing a number which is the number of bases you want to have in the chain. That is why our goal is efficiency at the moment of giving the result.
